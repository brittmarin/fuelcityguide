HOME THE GUIDE HOTSPOTS EVENTS

Once an old harbour area, ' 't Eilandje' is now a modern place where you can enjoy art, culture and gastronomy. It owes it's name to the docks that surround the MAS; Antwerp's largest museum that also brings a magnificent view over the city. Young businessmen consistently find their way to this sumptuous part of town. For this location provides the best joints for business lunches, meetings and parties.
With Fuel we want you to get to know the best restaurants and bars, the hottest clubs, the most unique art scenes and the most useful facilities. ' 't Eilandje' also finds yearly events that are an absolute must.
